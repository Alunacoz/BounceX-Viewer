Intiface / Device Control

Connect to Intiface Central to sync depth data with compatible devices (strokers, vibrators, etc.) while a video plays. Intiface Central must be running on the same machine or network.

Use ws://localhost:12345 if Intiface is on this machine, or ws://192.168.x.x:12345 for another device on your local network.
